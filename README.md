**The Hyper-Local Task Marketplace for the University of Oklahoma Community.**

## üìå Project Overview
SoonerGigs is a two-sided marketplace designed specifically for the OU ecosystem. It connects "Time-Poor" individuals (busy students, faculty, and grad students) with "Cash-Poor" students who have gaps in their schedules and need flexible, short-term work.

### The Problem
* **Rigid Employment:** Traditional campus jobs require fixed 15-20 hour weekly commitments.
* **Safety Concerns:** Existing platforms (Craigslist/FB Marketplace) are full of non-OU "townies" and scammers.
* **Friction:** Tasks like waiting in line at the Union, moving dorm furniture, or game-day errands have no dedicated platform.

### The Solution
A trust-based, **@ou.edu verified** mobile application that allows students to monetize their "micro-gaps" in time while providing affordable, reliable help to the campus community.
